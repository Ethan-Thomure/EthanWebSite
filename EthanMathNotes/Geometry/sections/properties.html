Generic Properties

Property 1
In the same circle or in congruent circles, all radii are equal and all diameters are equal
Property 2
If radii are equal, then the circles are congruent
Property 3
If centers of two congruent circles which lie in the same plane are made to coincide, the circles will coincide
Property 4
A point is within, on, or outside of a circle according to its distance from the center is less than, equal to, or greater than, the radius
Property 5
A straight line through any point within a circle cuts the circle at two points
Property 6
Within a given point as center and a given line as radius, one and only one circle can be drawn

Proportion Properties

Proportion 1
In any proportion the product of the means is equal to the product of the extremes
Proportion 2
If the product of two quantities is equal to the product of two other quantities, either pair can be used as means and the other pair extremes
Proportion 3
The mean proportion between two quantities is equal to the square root of their product
Proportion 4
The product of two or more proportions are proportional
Proportion 5
In series of equal ratios, the sum of antecedents, is to the sum of consequents as any antecedent is to its consequent
